History about the page

Chuck Norris. The Man. The Myth. The Legend. The actor, author and martial artist began acting in the 70s, alongside the likes of Dean Martin and Bruce Lee. By 1983 he made a name for himself in Lone McQuade, which inspired his later well-known lead role on Walker, Texas Ranger. With his expansive filmography, Norris has built a legend out of himself. Since the early 2000s, fans have crafted Norris jokes and fictional “facts”. They range from boasts about his roundhouse kicks, to how he is so powerful even the dark is afraid of him. The key to a decent Chuck Norris joke/fact is in its embellishment and sharp delivery. They are short and to the point and do not need any basis in real information about the actor. The simplicity makes each joke memorable and long-lasting. Although it is been years since his last acting role, Norris influence lives on as a long-running joke on the Internet.
